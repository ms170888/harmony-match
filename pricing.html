Frequently Asked Questions

How does the compatibility calculation work?

Our algorithm combines three key aspects of Chinese astrology: zodiac animal compatibility (50%), elemental interactions (35%), and yin-yang balance (15%). This weighted approach provides a comprehensive view of relationship dynamics.

What makes the paid tiers different?

While the free tier gives you basic compatibility scores, paid tiers provide deeper insights into relationship dynamics, practical advice, and personalized guidance. The Master and Ultimate tiers include comprehensive guides you can reference throughout your relationship.

Can I upgrade after getting a free reading?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time directly from your results page. Your birth year information will be preserved, and you'll get immediate access to the additional insights.

Is this based on real Chinese astrology?

Absolutely. Our system is based on traditional Chinese astrological principles including the 12 zodiac animals, 5 elements (Wu Xing), yin-yang theory, and compatibility groups like trines, allies, secret friends, and clashes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.
